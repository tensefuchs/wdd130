Overview

Purpose

The purpose of this website is:
- increase visibility of Whitewater Rafting to reach a wider audience
- market our rafting trips and adventures and share captivating photos and videos of rafting experiences
- be a hub for customers to browse our available rafting trips, check availability, and make reservations or bookings online
- share the different types of rafting trips we offer, safety measures, equipment, and what to expect during a rafting adventure - share reviews and Testimonials from satisfied customers

Audience

Our target audience for Whitewater rafting website consists of adventurous individuals and groups who seek outdoor experiences and are interested in exploring water-based adventures.
