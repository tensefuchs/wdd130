Overview

Purpose

My purpose is to expand my business and increasing my number of clients. I need this website to reach more customers and expand my area of potential customers. What I want to accomplish is: First, grow my business locallly and start spreading my website to my actual customers, by doing this I will be have a solid foundation of clients in my state. Second, start reaching out potential customers outside my State, by doing this I will expand and grow my business. It will benefit my company by having more reach and increasing the number of clients

Audience

My target customers are people interested in rafting activities, either individual or as a family. Their life situacion are customers with an age range of 13-40 years old, focused on customers with enough money to pay for rafting trips (basic salary or higher). They want to go out to rafting locations and practice this sport as individuals or as a family. Their needs not being met are buying rafting equipment, buying food or similar for the trip, tourism of places on our way to our rafting location. My audience will access to my website through desktop and mobile device
